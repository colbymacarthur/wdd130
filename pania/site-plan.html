Overview

Purpose

This website is to provide a landing spot for our clients to see current promotions, menu, and contact options. We want to bring a more personal experience for our social media followers and new potential clients. Later on it will serve as a platform to place and pay for orders for store pickup.

Audience

This website is for a gourmet bakery in a small town, our clients tend to be female 18-35 age range in the middle to upper class. We want a website that is optimized for cell phones with high resolution pictures to catch their attention.
